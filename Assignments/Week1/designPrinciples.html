Design Principles

Justin Hall

PARC: Contrast

The website for Celanese shows the value of using contrast. There is a great use of white and orange, with the orange sections drawing your eye to the center of the site. This is done to draw attention to the most important infomration the company wants you to know.

Visual Hierachy

YouTube is a video streaming platform that is designed to do one thing... watch videos. As such, the creators of the app have brought the video content front and center. Through this use of space and size, its easy for someone to know whats the most important.

White Space and Clean Design

Apples use of white space and a clean design means that your eyes go right to whats the most important. The color of the iPhone on the screen takes your eyes and mind right where they want you to be.
