Tours

Try Snorkeling

The ocean around Cozumel is beautiful, so jump in!
There are many places to go snorkeling that are accessable even from the shoreline,
or you can take a boat tour out to the amazing reefs.
Check out some of the many longer tours to the
deeper sections of the reefs to try and spot some of the larger species that call this water home.
Whatever you choose, you will be smiling the whole time.

Go Shopping

You can't make a trip to Mexico and not do some shopping,
and Cozumel is the perfect place to do just that. There is something for everyone in our beautiful city.
You may just end up buying your hammock from a local who has just finished stitching it
on his front stoop! Also, whether you have one day for shopping or many days, you must be sure to
visit Los Cinco Soles. Filled to the brim with stunning art, souvenirs, jewelry, clothing, traditional
handicrafts and more, Los Cinco Soles has something for everyone. It even has a tequila tasting bar!
All created by Mexican artisans, farmers and families. You will be so happy you visited.

Go on a Jeep Tour

Not all parts of Cozumel have good roads, so Jeep tours are a popular
way to see much more of the island. With trips through the woods and stops at beaches,
a Jeep Tour can be an excellent way to get the lay of the land on the island.
