Target Audience Description:

Texas Storm Watch is designed to be used by people of all ages, backgrounds and intrests. We are aiming to serve people in the DFW area
who need to know what the weather will be in that area.

Two Personas:

Person 1

Name: Carolyn
Age: 32
Location: Fort Worth, TX
Income: $89,000/annual
Family Status: Single
Interests: Heathly eating/food, fashion, contemporary arts
Use Case: Checks the weather before leaving the house each day.
Device(s): iPhone, MacBook


Person 2

Name: John
Age: 26
Location: Plano, TX
Income: $52,000/annual
Family Status: Single
Intrests: Photography, Hiking, Travel
Use Case: John checks the weather days in advance in order to plan his photoshoots and outdoor activities.
Device(s): iPad, Android Phone, Windows Laptop

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

A married banker wakes up every morning and checks Texas Storm Watch. Since he takes the light rail downtown each day for work, he needs to know if today
he will need to bring his umbrella. He can't let his papers get wet on the way to the office.

Scenario 2:

The winter is here and there are rumors of a winter storm blowing in. a single mother starts to check Texas Storm Watch to see
if she needs to turn up the heat in her home. She does not wanter her young child to catch a cold.

Scenario 3:

A teenage boy is trying to plan a perfect date to take his new girlfriend on. While cheacking Texas Storm Watch, he notices that the state fair is back in town!
He excitedly reads the announcment and sees that teh weather will be nice this saturaday. He clicks the link to buy tickets.

Scenario 4:

A mid twenties man is new to teh DFW area. Living in Fort Worth and working in Dallas, he takes the TRE train across the metro to get to work.
He checks the weather for the week on Mondays to decide if there will be any days this week that he will need to think about driving to the office instead.

Scenario 5:

The recent winter storm has been wrecking havok on the natual gas supply in the region. A new home owner, who has been without heat for several days
begins take a look at Texas Storm Watch to see if there are any updates on when there might be relief from the weather.
